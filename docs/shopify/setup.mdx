## Installation

- [ ] Find the "[Reachu](https://apps.shopify.com/outshifter?)" App in Shopify
      Marketplace.

<div class="homepage-section two-cols">
  <div class="section-content">
    <a class="homepage-card2">
      <div class="card-content">
        <div class="imagelogo">
          <img
            alt=""
            src="https://docs.reachu.io/static/integrations/ecommerce_logo_shopify.svg"
          />
        </div>
        <a href="https://apps.shopify.com/outshifter?">Reachu Shopify App</a>
      </div>
    </a>
  </div>
</div>

- [ ] Click **Add App**. (If you are not logged in to your Shopify store, do it
      now)
- [ ] Confirm the installation of the App by clicking **Install App**.
- [ ] Use your Reachu credentials to **log in** to the App.

:::info

Make sure you have created an Reachu account. To register for an account, please
click [here](https://dashboard.reachu.io/register)

:::

- [ ] Click the **Connect** button.

The product will then automatically appear in your Shopify, under the
**Product** section.
